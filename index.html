How the blend works: forecasts are blended from every source that has an opinion for the selected location — ECMWF, NOAA GFS, DWD ICON, UK Met Office, Météo-France, Environment Canada GEM & JMA via Open-Meteo, plus NWS/weather.gov in the US, MET Norway, and Bright Sky in Germany when available. NWS alerts show for US locations. Air quality and pollen use Open-Meteo when available. Radar tiles by RainViewer · basemap © OpenStreetMap & CARTO. Made with 🍋 for Lemons.
